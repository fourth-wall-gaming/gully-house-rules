\abstract{My in-house rules for running Mythras games in Greymyr, the world of Classic Fantasy. The goal is to pivot and subvert the d20 nature of Classic Fantasy back into the nuanced feel of Mythras, whilst still maintaining the core elements of a standard CF game. This is my attempt to add some additional flexibility to the CF worldview whilst still maintaining the core, fundamental nature of the game as an introduction to Mythras via an emulation of old-school ADnD.
